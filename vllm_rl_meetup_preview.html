In RL post-training,
inference becomes part
of the training loop.

vLLM stops being only a serving engine. It becomes a rollout engine for continuously changing models.

Classic serving Load once, answer requests

Stable model weights, request/response lifecycle, production-facing latency.

RL rollout engine Update often, generate training data

Fresh weights, rollout metadata, cache correctness, orchestration with trainers.

NEBIUS

From SFT to RLVR

The reward signal gets more operational.

SFT
Imitate examples

Train on curated demonstrations and target outputs.

RLHF
Optimize preferences

Use preference signals to push behavior toward human judgment.

RLVR
Optimize verifiable rewards

Math, code, and reasoning tasks expose scalable feedback through checks.

This is the bridge from model training to systems work: the training loop now needs a lot of generated behavior.
